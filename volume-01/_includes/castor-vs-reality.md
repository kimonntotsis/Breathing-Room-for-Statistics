### CASTOR versus reality

Each chapter’s CASTOR example is **deliberately orderly**. Before applying the same method to APATE, POLLUX, or your own registry, ask:

| CASTOR simplifies | Real analyst checks |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Clean visit windows and complete case files | Visit-window rules, protocol deviations, duplicate IDs |
| Single-site or balanced multi-centre design | Site clustering, equipment drift, training effects |
| Prespecified variables with few coding variants | EHR coding changes, unit errors, derived-variable logic |
| Moderate missingness with documented mechanism | Informative dropout, severity-related missing FEV₁ |
| Synthetic omics with intentional batch structure | Pre-analytical timing, platform versions, hemolysis |

**Exercise habit:** *The CASTOR analysis is correct for its estimand. What three additional checks are required before signing off APATE?*
