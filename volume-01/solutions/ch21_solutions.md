# Chapter 21 Solutions

**E21.1** Prior exacerbations, therapy adherence, occupational exposure, or baseline FEV1 (depending on estimand).

**E21.2** All common causes of exposure and outcome are measured and adjusted for; no unmeasured confounder remains.

**E21.3** Extreme weights dominate the pseudo-population and inflate variance; trimming and ESS matter.

**E21.4** Both: smoking affects FEV1 and FEV1 affects exacerbation. Adjusting FEV1 targets association not via lung function; omitting it targets total effect. Estimand must be prespecified.

**E21.5** Eligibility, treatment strategies, assignment mechanism, follow-up start, or outcome definition aligned to a hypothetical RCT.
