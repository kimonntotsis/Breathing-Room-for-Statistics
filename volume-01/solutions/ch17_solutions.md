# Chapter 17 Solutions

**E17.1** After Step 2 (batch QC): if group and batch are perfectly confounded, stop before interpreting DE hits.

**E17.2** With 1000 predictors, tuning λ on the same data you evaluate optimistically fits noise; nested CV separates tuning from performance estimation.

**E17.3** Tier 1 requires replicate ranking stability (3/3); a q < 0.05 protein is a single-modality statistical discovery without confirmation.

**E17.4** Cells from the same patient are correlated; pooling cells inflates *n* and can shrink p-values (Ch 15 pseudo-replication demo).

**E17.5** Examples: "validated diagnostic," "causal pathway proven," "ready for clinic deployment," "treatment should change based on panel."

**Applied** Use `ch17_integrated_pipeline_summary.csv`; draft Results with separate paragraphs per modality; avoid causal/diagnostic claims.
